Benefits of Attending SPAC as a Patron

SPAC is an excellent opportunity for companies to showcase their leading technologies and products to students, researchers and academics.

At SPAC, companies will also be able to network with the best and brightest engineering students in Ottawa for the possibility of a co-op work term, internship, research partnership, or future career.

By becoming a valuable patron for our event, SPAC 17 will be able to continue the success in helping students in the field of Engineering and Computer Science achieve their career goals

Patronage Options

Partner Patron* ($2000)

Patronage Includes

  • Ten (10) complimentary conference registrations
  • Three (3) Display tables in networking area
  • Space for banners in networking area, close to the entrance, and beside the main stage
  • Large logo on website and booklet
  • Individual display of logo on slide-show during the event
  • Public mention of patron during the welcome speech and closing remarks

*There is a maximum of one (1) Partner patron for this event.

Gold Patron ($1000)

Patronage Includes

  • Eight (8) complimentary conference registrations
  • Two (2) Display tables in networking area
  • Space for banners in networking area and close to the entrance
  • Medium-sized logo on website and booklet
  • Group display of logo on slide-show during the event

Silver Patron ($500)

Patronage Includes

  • Six (6) complimentary conference registrations
  • One (1) Display tables in networking area
  • Space for banner in networking area
  • Small-sized logo on website and booklet
  • Group display of logo on slide-show during the event

Bronze Patron ($300)

Patronage Includes

  • Three (3) complimentary conference registrations
  • Space for banner in networking area
  • Small-sized logo on website and booklet
  • Group display of logo on slide-show during the event

For a customized patronage package, contact the event chair at lead.director@ieeespac.ca
